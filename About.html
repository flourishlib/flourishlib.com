About

Flourish is an independent PHP library that I have developed during the past few years of my free time. Unsatisfied with the current options available for PHP frameworks and libraries, I took it upon myself to build one that would fulfill my needs. As the project progressed I made the decision to release it as open source, in the hope that it would save someone else from having to rewrite a similar solution.

If you are interested in the needs that Flourish helps fulfill, please check out the Motivations for Flourish page.
